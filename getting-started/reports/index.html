    How can my shop be a part of mzoori?

    In order to be a part of mzoori, you can contact us through mobile or email and we will give you support.

    First of all, we’ll need to verify that you have the following details:

    • Filled out mzoori retailer form
    • Any form of business registration- certificate of Incorporation/Business name/Business Permit
    • Copy of proprietor’s ID
    • KRA PIN certificate

    We will support you with:

    • Induction to and training on the mzoori platform
    • mzoori applications, software and information on approved gadgets
    • Access to a wide pool of pre-qualified mzoori partners to sell to directly or distribute your products to
    • Onboarding of any retail outlets, transporter or warehouse that you may nominate or appoint.

    Do you provide the equipment/POS system?

    Yes! We will install for you a fully automated and decentralized POS with the latest cutting-edge technology that enables you to manage your outlet from anywhere at anytime. It also enables you to continue sales even when offline. If you have your own equipment, we simply install the system for you, if you don’t have your own equipment, we provide you with the equipment as you pay for it gradually.

    How long will it take to be trained to use the system?

    Shouldn’t take long at all. The POS system is very user-friendly so that you’re able to navigate around it easily. We do offer you with training which may take upto 3 weeks, after which we offer you the support from our sales team who are just a call away.

    Will my products be secure?

    Making sure your products are safe is one of our key goals. Mzoori insures all the products across the supply chain upto the time the product is sold and every sale reported to you in realtime and payment to your bank account made immediately.Unlike other models, this totally eliminates the risk of making any losses as you can track your money throughout.

    Will my products be restocked in good time?

    Mzoori monitors your stock levels throughout and generates re-orders immediately your stock starts running low. You are therefore guaranteed that the deliveries will be made in good time and your shop will always be fully stocked.

    Will I be able to trace my products?

    Mzoori empowers you to monitor and track all your goods on-boarded onto the system from the warehouse to the retail store realtime, notifies you and settles your payments when they are sold.

    What mode of payment will we use?

    Retailpay enables retailers to accept payments from mobile money (mpesa), credit/debit cards and cash

    How fast are deliveries done after an order is made?

    Retailpay enables retailers to accept payments from mobile money (mpesa), credit/debit cards and cash

    How will Retail Pay benefit from the business?

    Retailpay will receive a convenience charge upon the sale of every products

    What are the requirements for sign-up?

    Mandatory:

    • Certificate of Incorporation/Business name/Business Permit
    • Company PIN Certificate
    • VAT Certificate
    • Names of Directors
    • Photocopy of proprietor(s) ID
    • Next of Kin ID
    • Memorandum and articles of Association
    • Letter of consent with bank details from director(s)
    • Photocopies of permanent staff ID's

    Where applicable:

    • Manufacturer's Licence, Business Permit
    • Policy on Market Returns
    • Ministry of Health Certification (food supplier)
    • Product Price list Inc. VAT and EX-VAT
    • KEBS Certificate
    • Distributorship Letter (Where necessary)
