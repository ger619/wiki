    Retailer App

    Managing stocktake just got easier. This app is a paperless inventory of all the products in the shop and is used during stocktake, which saves a lot of time compared to the actual physical process. It’s quick and easy to execute and requires very minimal investment.

    Features:

    • Manage buying price
    • Restock inventory
    • Manage offline suppliers
    • Receive mzoori stock
    • Make returns to suppliers
    • Adjust stock on server to match that of store

    Insurance Claims

    Claims can be raised automatically when the system detects disparities in stock - specifically if the quantity of physical stock in the store is less than that stipulated in the system.

    Every claim has to be approved by the store owner before it is raised. The insurance broker then investigates and decides if the claim should be settled. If so, they approve and the supplier is paid immediately from the performance bond account. The retail inventory is also adjusted to remove the quantity of items recorded in the claim.

    If the insurance broker’s investigation reveals fraud on the part of the retailer, the claim is still settled from the performance bond account but the retailer is held accountable to refunding the account. If the claim is genuine the broker follows up with any of the insurance companies responsible for settlement of the claim.

    If the claim is settled by the insurance company the money is deposited in the counter indemnity account. The insurance broker also approves closing of the claim. This invokes settlement from the counter indemnity account to the performance bond account.

    • Performance bond
      • The performance bond consists of funds set aside by retailpay to be paid to suppliers in form of claims
    • Counter indemnity
      • The counter indemnity is managed by insurance and is used to replenish the performance bond after claims are issued. Insurance also has to approve all claims before replenishing the performance bond.

    Suppliers- Supplier relations

    Mzoori offers solutions for suppliers who face several challenges such as payments never being made on time, affecting liquidity. Some of the solutions are available on the web portal which has the following features:

    • Check their inventory
    • Check for orders from warehouse
    • See the number of stores stocking their products
    • Withdraw cash from the current account to their bank

    Mzoori assists suppliers to conduct their business smoothly and with peace of mind. The system gets rid of any inconveniences such as bad debts since settlements are made real time after every sale.
